Frequently asked questions

Subscription and Payment

Does the subscription auto-renew?

Yes, the subscription is automatically renewed every year, and it is charged from the credit card saved on your account.

Can I cancel at anytime?

Yes, you may cancel the subscription from your account settings. The subscription will remain valid until the last date of your billing cycle, but will not renew.

What does "free updates" mean?

The component library will be frequently updated and new items will be added periodically (new marketing or app components, pages, etc). Once you purchase a license you get all these updates for free as long as you have an active subscription.

If your subscription ends and you choose to not renew it, you will still get lifetime access to everything you got until that moment.

What does "lifetime access" mean?

We want to keep our pricing models simple, so we decided to create 3 simple pricing plans (billed annually) to cover most of the use-cases: the Personal, Pro, and Team.

Each license is perpetual, meaning that you'll still have a license if you don't renew your paid plan. You'll retain access to the last version available when your plan was active.

What license is best for my entire team?

The Team Plan was specially created to cover the needs of larger projects with many users involved (up to 5 seats). This means that 5 individuals who are in your organization or are clients can create websites with the components and features on any number of projects, including commercial or SaaS websites.

If you need to increase this number of seats, please contact us for a customized offer.

What version of Bootstrap is used?

Our CSS framework and all the components are built on versions of Bootstrap v5. As more Bootstrap updates are launched the products will be updated as needed and as new features and bug fixes come out. You will want to download any updates that come out and update your installation as required.

What browsers are supported?

We support the latest, stable releases of all major browsers and platforms. On Windows, we support Internet Explorer 10-11 / Microsoft Edge.

Alternative browsers which use the latest version of WebKit, Blink, or Gecko, whether directly or via the platform‚Äôs web view API, are not explicitly supported.

Components and Templates

What JS framework is used?

We want to make all our products as universal as possible in order to make them easier to use, and also ensure compatibility with other frameworks or technologies. This is why we don't make use of any third-party JS framework

However, since the CSS framework and the components are based on Bootstrap 5, you can bring your website to life with the optional JavaScript plugins included in Bootstrap. Switching from jQuery to JavaScript will allow developers to integrate Bootstrap with any JS framework, without worrying about the compatibility or errors that might occur during the process.

Learn more about each Bootstrap plugin, data and programmatic API options, and more.

How often do you make updates?

We update all of our products with each Bootstrap update, plus we are constantly adding new components, pages, and features to our library periodically.

This is just the very beginning of what is yet to come. Plenty of ideas are already pending while we are working on the next batch of components and features. Make sure to follow us on Twitter or subscribe to our newsletter to be the first to find out.

Do you have a community channel?

Yes, we do. Even though it was recently created, our goal is to create everything around our community's suggestions and feedback. With each purchase you make you get access to our private Discord server where you will be able to get answers to your questions, suggest new features we should add, or share valuable tips with the community.

New features mostly start as requests by the Webpixels community. If you don't find a component you need, feel free to request it on our Discord.

How many projects can I build with each purchase?

You can create unlimited websites or apps for your clients, transfer the website to your client or charge them for your services. You don't have to buy a new license every time you want to use it on a new project.

For more information read our license.

Can I build my own commercial projects?

Yes, you can use the components to build commercial websites or SaaS platforms where you charge users. The Team Plan was created to cover this specific need. Buy once and use it on an unlimited number of commercial projects for you or your clients.

Can I build open-source projects?

Yes, you can include using our components in your open-source project as long as you don't use them in any application allowing users to customize the components, like ‚Äúbuild it yourself‚Äù, ‚Äútheme generators‚Äù or similar component libraries.

For more information read our license.

Can I sell templates/themes I build with Webpixels products?

No, you cannot build and sell any kind of products that includes our components on any platform/marketplace, except to your client.

If you have a great design eye and want to create a theme using our components, feel free to contact us anytime.

How can I request a refund?

Sure, you have 15 days to evaluate your purchase. If your purchase fails to meet expectations, or is critically flawed in some way, contact us and we will issue a full refund pending a review.

What if I have a question that isn't answered here?

For anything, we haven't covered feel free to reach out to the Webpixels team at support@webpixels.io! We're here to help.
